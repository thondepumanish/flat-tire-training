How to Change a Flat Tire

Master the skill of changing a flat tire with this step-by-step guide.

Jump to a step:

Step 1: Safety First

Pull over safely, turn on hazard lights, apply parking brake, and use wheel wedges.

Step 2: Gather Tools

Spare tire, jack, lug wrench, car manual, gloves, and flashlight.

Step 3: Loosen Lug Nuts

Use a lug wrench to loosen the lug nuts before jacking up the car.

Step 4: Jack Up the Car

Position the jack under the vehicleâ€™s frame and lift the car until the flat tire is off the ground.

Step 5: Remove and Replace the Tire

Completely remove the lug nuts, take off the flat tire, and replace it with the spare.

Step 6: Tighten Lug Nuts and Lower the Car

Tighten the lug nuts in a crisscross pattern, lower the car, and ensure all nuts are secure.
